Reflections on the Zine

I created this website zine for Saint Wilgefortis because I enjoy making websites and felt it would be a great medium to work with, giving me plenty of creative freedom. Learning about this saint was fascinating. Even though I attended Catholic school for nearly ten years, I had never heard of her before. The history behind her story really stood out to me, and I found it both intriguing and thought-provoking.

Along with the website, I wrote a short poetry piece reflecting on how her transformation is a blessing. While zines are often colorful with a collage-like feel, I chose an aesthetic that matched the atmosphere I felt while reading about her. It may not follow the traditional zine style, but I believe it captures the essence of her story in a way that feels right.
